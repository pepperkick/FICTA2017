The aim of this 3rd International Conference on Smart Computing & Informatics (SCI-2018) is to present a unified platform for advanced and multi-disciplinary research towards design of smart computing and informatics. The theme is on a broader front focuses on various innovation paradigms in system knowledge, intelligence and sustainability that may be applied to provide realistic solution to varied problems in society, environment and industries. The scope is also extended towards deployment of emerging computational and knowledge transfer approaches, optimizing solutions in varied disciplines of science, technology and healthcare.The conference proceeding aims publication of quality and unpublished work pertaining to specific topics of interest as mentioned in CFP.
