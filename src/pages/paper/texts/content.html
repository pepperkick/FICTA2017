3rd International Conference on Smart Computing & Informatics (SCI-2018) will provide an excellent international forum for sharing knowledge and experimental results in theory, methodology and applications of Smart Computing & Informatics. The Conference looks for significant contributions to all major fields of the Computer Science and Information Technology in theoretical and practical aspects.
